  • The fee for rental of one (1) graphing calculator of make and model TI-83/ TI-83+/ TI-84 / TI-84+ is $30. The fee for rental one (1) graphing calculator of make and model CASIO fx-9750GII is $20. A copy of the receipt will be given to you.
  • If you rent a TI-83/ TI-83+/ TI-84 / TI-84+, by the end of the semester when you return the calculator back to the LOCATION you will get $10 back.
  • If you rent a CASIO fx-9750GII, by the end of the semester when you return the calculator back to the LOCATION you will get $10 back.
  • The rental period lasts until the end of the course.
  • The graphical calculator must be returned within a week of final exams for the course. If the calculator is not returned by the end of finals exam week, a phone call will be made to you from a lab assistant to remind you to return the calculator on following Monday. If the calculator is not returned by Monday, steps will be taken immediately to put a hold on your final grade.
